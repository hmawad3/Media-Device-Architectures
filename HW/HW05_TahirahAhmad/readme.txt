========================
The Game
By Tahirah Ahmad
========================

Game:
This is the apple catching game but just with different objects.
You play as three little men who have to run back and forth to collect
20 hearts because they don't have life insurance and this will give it to them.
Avoid the angry brown squares that descend as they will lower your health!

The three men can shoot bullets from the white hat they are wearing to
knock down the angry squares. Be careful though, if you hit a heart, you'll
lose health!

If your health falls to 0, you will die!!

------------------------

Controls:
Use the right and left arrow keys to move your men.
Press A to fire bullets.
Hit Start to pause the game and to resume the game.
If you lost or won and would like to play again, hit start
